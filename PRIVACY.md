# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 18, 2026

## Our Philosophy
HyperZHub is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you.  
We do not design our products to collect, store, or monetize personal information—period.

## What We Do NOT Do
We explicitly do NOT:
- Track you
- Profile you
- Sell your data
- Store your personal information
- Monitor your browsing activity
- Use analytics or behavioral tracking tools

## HyperZHub Browser
The HyperZHub Browser is designed to keep your activity on your device.

- Your browsing history stays local
- We do not transmit your activity to our servers
- We do not create user identifiers or fingerprints
- We do not sync or back up your data unless explicitly implemented and clearly disclosed

### Necessary Functionality
To function as a web browser, HyperZHub must handle:
- Website requests
- Domain lookups
- Network communication (including IP address exchange)

This is inherent to how the internet works and is **not collected, stored, or controlled by us**.

## HyperZHub Website
When you visit our website:

- No tracking scripts are used
- No analytics platforms are embedded
- No advertising technologies are present

### Minimal Technical Processing
Our hosting provider may automatically process limited technical information such as:
- IP address
- Request metadata (time, browser type)

This data is:
- Used only to deliver and secure the service
- Not accessed, analyzed, or stored by us beyond what is strictly necessary

## Cookies
We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

Any cookies, if ever used, will be:
- Strictly necessary
- Short-lived
- Clearly disclosed

## Third-Party Infrastructure
We rely on external infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers) to deliver our services. These providers may handle minimal technical data as part of standard internet operations.

We do not integrate third-party trackers, analytics, or data brokers.

## Data Retention
We do not retain personal data because we do not collect it.

Any unavoidable technical logs generated by infrastructure are:
- Automatically rotated or deleted
- Not used for identification or profiling

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with anyone.

## Security
We design our systems to minimize data exposure by default. The best way to protect your data is not to collect it in the first place.
